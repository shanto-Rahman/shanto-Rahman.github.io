I am Shanto Rahman, a final-year Ph.D. student in Software Engineering and Systems at the University of Texas at Austin, advised by August Shi. My research interests are at the intersection of Software Engineering and Artificial Intelligence, with a focus on Testing of Software Systems and AI Models to make them robust, reliable, and safe.

I design and build tools that make large-scale software systems more reliable and easier to test.
