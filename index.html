About Me

Welcome to my page! I am Shanto Rahman, a 4th year Ph.D. student in Software Engineering and Systems at the University of Texas at Austin. My research focuses on software testing and reliability in AI/ML systems. I work with August Shi and collaborate with Baishakhi Ray, Sasa Misailovic, and Wing Lam.

Previously, I was a Senior Software Engineer at Samsung Research in Bangladesh and a Lecturer at Bangladesh University of Professionals (BUP). I hold an M.Sc. and B.Sc. from the University of Dhaka.

My work has appeared in top venues including ICSE, OOPSLA, ASE, and ICST, and I’ve applied these methods during internships at Google and AWS.
