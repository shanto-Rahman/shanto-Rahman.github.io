Welcome to my page! I am Shanto Rahman, a fourth-year Ph.D. student in Software Engineering and Systems at the University of Texas at Austin. My research focuses on software testing and reliability in any software systems. I work with August Shi and closely collaborate with Baishakhi Ray, Sasa Misailovic, and Wing Lam. I completed two research internships at Google and Amazon Web Service (AWS).

Previously, I worked as a Senior Software Engineer at Samsung Research in Bangladesh. After working one year there, I joined in Bangladesh University of Professionals as a faculty member where I teach different software engineering courses. I obtained my M.Sc. and a B.Sc. from the University of Dhaka.

My work has been published in top-tier venues, including ICSE, OOPSLA, ASE, and ICST.
